The Quest

The cloud storage and organization market is young and ripe for development.
Even with big name companies filling the space there is room for another
player to meet specific needs and enter the fray. Identifying a need in an
already competitive market provided a challenge to overcome. The client
identified there is potential to do something new in the cloud storage market
but did not give much information beyond that. It was up to me, the UX
researcher and designer to laser focus my efforts by gathering data and
massaging it into a product design to bring to market.

For the main logo I did a preference test to identify if a black outline should be
included on the main logo or if white lettering worked better before beginning the
process of working on High Fidelity output.

Preference testing showed users preferred white text in the main logo.

Preference testing did not conclusively show any difference when testing rounded
corners for buttons to use within the app so I stuck with my initial choice
for corners.
