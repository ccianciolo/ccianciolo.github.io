Logos and Branding

SchoolGroup

For the potential redesign of the SchoolGroup logo I wanted to create something that was simple, minimalistic, and connected education to the SchoolGroup concept. Innovation, quality, accessible, authoritative, trustworthy and insightful. All of these characteristics define SchoolGroup as an e-Learning titan in the educational space. This logo concept was designed with current brand guidelines in mind to illustrate these characteristics and create something memorable and impactful.

Cookie Crumbs

Cookie Crumbs with Maggie was a passion project done for a local artisan baker in Alfred, ME. The client gave some inspiration for the logo design and wanted to illustrate the key terms home, warm, sweet, delicious, and local. Color choices were styled after a chocolate chip cookie which is one of the original items that were offered in this local bakery.

TopSpin

The TopSpin logo was designed to invoke the memory and feeling of being on a tennis court and playing tennis. Colors were selected after the court colors of Wimbledon and the French open. The complementary fonts Bebas Neue and DIN Condensed were chosen because they are clean, blocky fonts that invoke old style tennis signage from the past. The overarching idea of the app is to be simple to use and to bring improvement to players of any level. The icons, colors, and typography work to do just that.

Self Branding

Branding for the Charlie Cianciolo Design website was done as a true UX case study. Keywords used to design the logo were clean, symmetrical , simple, and refined. This logo was designed using the client's face with a sketch of one side of his portrait which was then mirrored onto the other side. The smirk was chosen to portray some playfulness and to invoke his trademark look.
