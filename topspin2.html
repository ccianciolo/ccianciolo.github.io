TopSpin

Growing up in Maine focusing on tennis as a sport is a challenge. Cost aside, tennis is an easy sport to get into, but is difficult to master. Tennis coaches in the area guide students to use a tennis notebook to gather notes and keep track of their daily goals but it’s a challenge to get this routine to stick. TopSpin Cloud meets the needs of both coaches and players alike by allowing for collaboration and tracking of progress in an easy to use iOS application.

Problem

Tennis coaches in the area face inconsistent weather conditions and meeting times during the Spring season which leads to a decrease in overall improvement. Coaches guide students to use a tennis notebook to gather notes and keep track of their daily goals but it’s a challenge to get this routine to stick.

Solution

TopSpin aims to meet the needs of both coaches and players alike by allowing for collaboration and tracking of progress in an easy to use iOS application. TopSpin is mobile so it can go anywhere, capture info on training, and allow for the ever Important communication between player and coach.

What I learned

I had doubts at the beginning about whether or not another cloud storage service would fit into the already saturated market. The initial user survey did not verify that the need, was in fact, there. But my instinct said to press on. A second, more focused survey (which focused on tennis players and coaches) shed some more light on the direction of the application and its overall design. Given more time, I would have done some in-person interviews with potential users to identify what types of features would make V1 of the application successful. This research really determined the direction of the app and any processes and design stemmed from it. Details, details, details are what I learned from designing this application. I learned a lot about design systems while designing this app. I would utilize this knowledge to make the future design more streamlined and simplified. All of the initial information gathered directed decisions I made throughout the process. The end result is a research-based design that can be implemented for design onto the iOS app store.
